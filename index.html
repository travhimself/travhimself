about me

My name is Travis Sisti. I design and build stuff with my Mac. I'm equal parts left brain and right brain.

I was raised in upstate NY and studied corporate finance at Stonehill College outside Boston. After graduation I spent three years in Washington DC, then moved to Santa Monica, CA.

I put 1200 miles on my bike last year after moving to LA without a car, and frickin' loved it. (Although I do have a car now.)

I've played the drums for 15+ years; everything from rock to jazz to orchestral to marching.

I drink lots and lots of loose-leaf tea. Mostly from Samovar.

I recently took up Brazilian jiu jitsu ('the flexible art') to stay in shape and keep my mind limber.

I spend the rest of my time in front of a computer.

This summer I'm at TechStars Boulder working as HackStar, lending my design and front-end chops to a bunch of awesome startups.

If I was a character in a game, my stats would look something like this:

I'm 100% self-taught.
